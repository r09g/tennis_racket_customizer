Swingweight

SWnew = SWoriginal + (m / 1000) × (d − 10)²

Moment of inertia about the RDC pivot (10 cm from butt). Added mass contributes via the parallel axis theorem. Units: kg·cm².

Assumptions & Constraints

  • Racket length: 68.58 cm (27 inches, fixed)
  • RDC pivot axis: 10 cm from butt
  • Lead tape is modeled as a point mass at the chosen position
  • Swingweight can only increase (adding mass always adds ≥ 0 to moment of inertia)
  • Balance moves toward head or handle depending on tape position relative to current balance
